Diverse Ecosystems Sri Lanka


The island has a diverse range of ecosystems. Inland wetlands, forests,grasslands, and coastal and marine habitats are the main types of natural ecosystems found in the nation. Agricultural environments are included as well.There are many kinds of forests: dry mixed evergreen forests, dry thorn forests, and wet evergreen forests (both lowland and montane). Low country and the montane areas are home to grasslands. A complicated system of rivers and freshwater bodies is a part of inland wetlands.
